*Forced march/ride means that no natural healing will occur while traveling.
**Death march/ride involves an actual risk to one’s life and wellbeing.
All who travel at this speed suffer at least 1 point of Toughness each day, and must make a Strong test in order to not suffer an additional 1D6 points of damage. A roll with the outcome 20 means that the traveler has been mortally damaged in an accident during its journey.
